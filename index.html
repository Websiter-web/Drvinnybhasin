Meet The Expert

Dr. Vinny Bhasin

Dr. Vinny Bhasin

MDS - Orthodontics, BDS

Dr. Vinny Bhasin is a highly respected Orthodontist and Dentist with an overall experience of 19 years (17 years strictly as a specialist). Known for his polite nature, patience, and professional precision, he has successfully transformed countless smiles.

Operating exclusively out of his Nagar Nigam clinic with no branches, he ensures that every patient receives his undivided, expert attention from the first consultation to the final result.

🎓 MDS (Orthodontics): Rajiv Gandhi University Of Health Sciences, Bangalore (2007)

🎓 BDS: Bharti Vidyapeeth Dental College, Pune (1990)

📜 Registration: A-2186 Madhya Pradesh State Dental Council (Verified)

Where does Dr. Vinny Bhasin practice? +

Dr. Vinny Bhasin practices exclusively at Bhasin Dental Clinic & Orthodontic Centre located in Tibadewala Market, Karamchand Chowk, Nagar Nigam, Jabalpur. Please note that there are no other branches.

Why do patients visit Dr. Vinny Bhasin? +

Patients frequently visit Dr. Vinny Bhasin for highly specialized Orthodontic care (Braces/Aligners), Root Canal Treatments (RCT), expert Dental Fillings, and painless Surgical Extractions. He is highly rated for his polite nature and precision.

What are Dr. Vinny Bhasin's educational qualifications? +

How many years of experience does he have? +

Dr. Vinny Bhasin has an overall experience of 19 years in dentistry, including 17 dedicated years as a registered Orthodontic specialist.
